title: "Inventory"
---

The Inventory feature is a solution for you, as a Media Owner, to manage all your billboard or display assets more effectively and in a structured way. This feature allows users to track, manage, and monitor all their inventory assets in one place.

This page will help you understand the various menus within the Inventory feature.

### Accessing Inventory Display

1. Click "**Inventory**" on the Navigation Bar.

   <img
     src="/images/AccessingInventoryDisplay-1.png"
     alt="Accessing Inventory Display 1 Pn"
     title="Accessing Inventory Display 1 Pn"
     style={{ width:"97%" }}
   />

   <div />
2. Click the "**Display**" menu.

   ![Accessing Inventory Display 2 Pn](/images/AccessingInventoryDisplay-2.png)

   <div />

   This will bring you to the Inventory Display page, which shows a list of your inventories.

   <div />

   ![Accessing Inventory Display 2 2 Pn](/images/AccessingInventoryDisplay-2.2.png)
