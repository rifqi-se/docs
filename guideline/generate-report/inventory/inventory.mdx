title: "Inventory"

The Inventory feature is a solution for you, as a Media Owner, to manage all your billboard or display assets more effectively and in a structured way. This feature allows users to track, manage, and monitor all their inventory assets in one place.

This page will help you understand the various menus within the Inventory feature.
