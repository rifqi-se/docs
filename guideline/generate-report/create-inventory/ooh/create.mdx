---
title: "Creating a OOH Inventory"
---

This page will help you create or register OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory on the Lynx SSP Platform.

Creating a OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory consists of several steps, including:

## Accessing the Add New Inventory Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Add New' Inventory Button">
    ![Accessingthe Add New Inventory Page 1 Pn](/images/AccessingtheAddNewInventoryPage-1.png)

    This will take you to the **Add New Inventory Page**, where you can fill out a form to create a new inventory item.

    ![Fire Shot Capture218 Inventory Display Add Old Client Portal [dashboard Lynxssp Com] Pn](/images/FireShotCapture218-InventoryDisplayAddOld-ClientPortal-[dashboard.lynxssp.com].png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create a OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory Form

When adding a OOH (Out-of-Home) inventory to the Lynx SSP Platform, you'll need to prepare and enter several pieces of information or data into the following form:

![Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form Pn](/images/MembuatOOH\(Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm.png)

Based on the form above, here is the information and data required to add a new **OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory**:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Display Name (Required)" iconType="regular" titleSize="p">
    You can enter the **Display Name** or a unique name for your OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory.

    <img
      src="/images/MembuatOOH(Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-1.png"
      alt="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 1 Pn"
      title="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 1 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="SKU (Required)">
    There are no strict rules for SKU numbering. The format and numbering are entirely up to your company's policy. The SKU can be used as a unique parameter to perform a specific search for an inventory item.

    <img
      src="/images/MembuatOOH(Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-2.png"
      alt="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 2 Pn"
      title="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 2 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Venue ID (Optional)">
    **Venue ID** is a unique identification code used to mark the location or placement point of an inventory item (like a billboard or DOOH screen) within the Lynx SSP. The Venue ID makes it easier for the system to differentiate one digital screen from another, even if they are in the same area.

    <img
      src="/images/MembuatOOH(Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-3.png"
      alt="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 3 Pn"
      title="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 3 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Specification (Required)">
    You can select the specification that matches your OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory. (See Specification, Type & Category)

    Example: Choose the Roadside specification.

    ![Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 4 Pn](/images/MenambahkanDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-4.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type (Required)">
    You select the "OOH" type because you are adding a OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory.

    ![Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 5 Pn](/images/MembuatOOH\(Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-5.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Category (Required)">
    You must select the appropriate category for the OOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory you are adding.

    For example, choose the Conventational Billboard category.

    ![Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 6 Pn](/images/MembuatOOH\(Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-6.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lighting (Required)">
    When you select Conventional Billboard (OOH) as the billboard type & category, you must provide information on the type of lighting used on the billboard. The available options include:

    <Accordion title="Front Light">
      The lighting is positioned in front of the billboard media and shines onto it.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Backlight">
      The lighting is positioned behind the billboard media and shines through it.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="No Light">
      The billboard has no lighting at all.
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Undefined">
      Not defined.
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Height (Required)">
    You must enter the height of the OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory you are adding. The unit used is meters.

    For example, if you choose a Conventional Billboard with dimensions of 4 m x 3 m and a height from the ground of 3,5 m, you would enter 3 m as the height.

    <img
      src="/images/MembuatOOH(Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-8.png"
      alt="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 8 Pn"
      title="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 8 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Width (Required)">
    You must enter the width of the OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory you are adding. The unit used is meters.

    For example, if you choose a Conventional Billboard with dimensions of 4 m x 3 m and a height from the ground of 3,5 m, you would enter 4 m as the width.

    <img
      src="/images/MembuatOOH(Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-9.png"
      alt="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 9 Pn"
      title="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 9 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Height from Ground (Required)">
    You must enter the height from the ground of the OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory you are adding. The unit used is meters.

    For example, if you choose a Conventional Billboard with dimensions of 4 m x 3 m and a height from the ground of 3,5 m, you would enter 3,5 m as the height from the ground.

    <img
      src="/images/MembuatOOH(Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-10.png"
      alt="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 10 Pn"
      title="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 10 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Location (Latitude & Longitude) (Required)">
    You must enter the location points, including the latitude and longitude, of your Out-of-Home (OOH) Inventory.

    For example, if your Conventional Billboard's location is at -6.224694331660831, 106.80518378102471, you would enter those coordinates.

    ![Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 11 Pn](/images/MembuatOOH\(Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-11.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Direction Facing (Optional)">
    It is highly recommended that you set the Direction Facing for your inventory. Direction Facing provides information on which direction your billboard is facing.

    This information adds significant value for potential buyers, as it helps them determine the optimal ad placement for their campaigns.

    <img
      src="/images/MembuatOOH(Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-12.png"
      alt="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 12 Pn"
      title="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 12 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Direction Facing Method & Angle (Required)">
    Once you have enabled Direction Facing, you must enter information regarding the method used and its range. This information is crucial for providing accurate data to potential advertisers, allowing them to better evaluate the effectiveness of your ad placement.

    ![Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 13 Pn](/images/MembuatOOH\(Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-13.png)

    You can use either Custom Direction or Cardinal Direction.

    <Accordion title="Custom Direction">
      If you're using Custom Direction, you'll need to enter the Latitude and Longitude of the location, along with the Visibility Distance in meters.

      ![Screenshot2025 10 15075701 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-10-15075701.png)
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Cardinal Direction">
      If using Cardinal Direction, you choose which way the billboard faces, along with the Visibility Distance or its range (in meters).

      ![Screenshot2025 10 15073431 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-10-15073431.png)
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map Location Billboard">
    After entering the Location information (Latitude & Longitude) and the Direction Facing, the map will display the specific location of your OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard.

    ![Screenshot2025 10 15074104 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-10-15074104.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Address Notes (Required)">
    You can fill in this note with the complete address of your inventory location.

    Example: You chose the **Conventional Billboard** at Blok M 150, South Jakarta, Jakarta.

    ![Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 15 Pn](/images/MembuatOOH\(Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-15..png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Potential impressions per day (Optional)">
    If you have data on the number of potential impressions per day generated by that inventory, you can fill in this column. If not, you can leave it blank.

    Example: You chose the Digital Billboard with a potential impression of 200,000 per day.

    ![Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 16 Pn](/images/MembuatOOH\(Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-16..png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Display Image (Optional)">
    You can upload photos of the OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard you are adding to Lynx SSP.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Display Status (Optional)">
    By default, the billboard status is set to **False**. If you activate this status, the billboard will appear in the inventory list that can be selected when running a campaign and will be available for sale.

    <img
      src="/images/MembuatOOH(Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-18.png"
      alt="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 18 Pn"
      title="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 18 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Price (Required)">
    You must enter the monthly price for the OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard you are adding to Lynx SSP.

    Example: You chose the Conventional Billboard with a price of IDR 100,000,000 per month.

    ![Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 19 Pn](/images/MembuatOOH\(Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-19.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Display Operational Days (Optional)">
    You can select the specific days of operation for the OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard you are adding to Lynx SSP. By default, it will be displayed every day.

    <img
      src="/images/MembuatOOH(Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-20.png"
      alt="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 20 Pn"
      title="Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 20 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Display Operational Hours (Optional)">
    You can select the specific operational hours for the OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard that you are adding to Lynx SSP. By default, it will be displayed for all 24 hours.

    ![Membuat Ooh(out Of Home)inventory Form 21 Pn](/images/MembuatOOH\(Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-21.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Save' ">
    After filling in all the information for adding a OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory, press the "Save" button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After completing the steps to create a **OOH (Out-of-Home) Inventory**, you can use that inventory in a campaign and media plan to generate a campaign report.
