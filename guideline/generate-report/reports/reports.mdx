title: "Reports"

**Reports** are a feature on the Lynx SSP Platform that provides performance reporting for client campaigns during their run. When a campaign concludes, clients who have placed advertisements on your inventory can obtain detailed reports on the achievements generated by those campaigns.

This page will help you understand the various sections available in the **Campaign Reports Feature**.
