---
title: "Reports"
---

**Reports** are a feature on the Lynx SSP Platform that provides performance reporting for client campaigns during their run. When a campaign concludes, clients who have placed advertisements on your inventory can obtain detailed reports on the achievements generated by those campaigns.

This page will help you understand the various sections available in the **Campaign Reports Feature**.

---

### Filter by Date Range
Filters data based on the campaign schedule.  
By **default**, the selected range is **1 month backward** (if the campaign period exceeds 1 month).  
([See Creating a Campaign ↗](/guideline/generate-report/create-campaign/create))

<img src="/reports-filter-by-date-range.gif" alt="Filter by Date Range" className="mx-auto" style={{ width: "100%" }} />

---

### Filter by Media Plan
Filters data based on **Media Plan**.  
If the campaign includes multiple media plans, you can choose a specific one.

<img src="/reports-filter-by-media-plan.gif" alt="Filter by Media Plan" className="mx-auto" style={{ width: "100%" }} />

---

### Filter by City & District
Filters data based on the **City or District** of the inventory point.

<img src="/reports-filter-by-city-district.gif" alt="Filter by City and District" className="mx-auto" style={{ width: "100%" }} />

---

### Filter by Inventory
Filters data based on the specific **Inventory** you want to view.

<img src="/images/Overview-7.png" alt="Filter by Inventory" className="mx-auto" style={{ width: "35%" }} />

### Filter Menu
Use the filter menu to refine campaign reporting data. Available filters include:

- **Date Range**
- **Media Plan**
- **Inventory**
- **Display Type**
- **Creative**
- **Inventory Size**

<img src="/images/Overview-8.png" alt="Filter Menu" className="mx-auto" style={{ width: "20%" }} />

<img src="/images/image.png" alt="Filter Menu Dropdown Example" className="mx-auto" style={{ width: "27%" }} />

Once done, click **Apply Filter** to update the report.

---

### Filtering Search Button
After applying filters, click this button to refresh the data according to your selections.
