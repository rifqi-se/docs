**Reports** are a feature on the Lynx SSP Platform that provides performance reporting for client campaigns during their run. When a campaign concludes, clients who have placed advertisements on your inventory can obtain detailed reports on the achievements generated by those campaigns.

This page will help you understand the various sections available in the **Campaign Reports Feature**.

### Share Campaign Report Button

This button is used to generate a web link that you can then provide to your clients, allowing them to access the campaign report directly via a web browser ([See Share Campaign Report ↗](/guideline/generate-report/reports/share)).

![Reports Share Campaign Report Button Pn](/images/reports/reports/reports-share-campaign-report-button.png)

---

### Download Campaign Report Button

This button is used to **download** the desired campaign report. The successfully downloaded report will be in **PDF file** format ([See Download Campaign Report ↗](/guideline/generate-report/reports/download)).

![Reports Download Campaign Report Button Pn](/images/reports/reports/reports-download-campaign-report-button.png)

---

### Filter by Date Range

The date range that can be used on this page is the range from the campaign schedule you previously applied ([See Creating a Campaign ↗](/guideline/generate-report/create-campaign/create)). By **default**, the selected date range in this filter is **1 month backward** (if the campaign period is longer than 1 month).

![Reports Filter By Date Range Pn](/images/reports/reports/reports-filter-by-date-range.png)

---

### Filter by Media Plan

The data displayed on this page can be filtered based on the **Media Plan**. If a single campaign contains multiple media plans, you can specifically choose to display the campaign report only for a certain **Media Plan.**

![Reports Filter By Media Plan Pn](/images/reports/reports/reports-filter-by-media-plan.png)

---

### Filter by City & District

The data displayed on this page can be filtered based on the **City or District** where the Inventory point is located.

![Reports Filter By City And District Pn](/images/reports/reports/reports-filter-by-city-and-district.png)

---

### Filter by Inventory

The data displayed on this page can be filtered based on the specific **Inventory** item you want.

![Reports Filter By Inventory Pn](/images/reports/reports/reports-filter-by-inventory.png)

---

### Filter Menu

Through the filter menu, you can perform filtering or **refining of campaign reporting data** to better suit your needs. The filters available include:

- **Filter by Date Range**
- **Filter by Media Plan**
- **Filter by Inventory**
- **Filter by Display Type**
- **Filter by Creative**
- **Filter by Inventory Size**

![Reports Filter Menu 1 Pn](/images/reports/reports/reports-filter-menu-1.png)

Once you have finished filtering, press the **'Apply Filter'** button, and the campaign report will display data based on your specific requirements.

![Launch Media Plan 2 Pn](/images/LaunchMediaPlan-2.png)

---

### Filtering Search Button

After you have performed the filtering (Points No. 4, 5, 6, and 7), you can press this search button, and the campaign report will display data based on your specific requirements.
