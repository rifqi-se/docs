---
title: "Creating a DOOH Inventory"
---

This page will help you create or register DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory on the Lynx SSP Platform.

Creating a DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory consists of several steps, including:

## Accessing the Add New Inventory Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Add New' Inventory Button">
    ![Accessingthe Add New Inventory Page 1 Pn](/images/AccessingtheAddNewInventoryPage-1.png)

    This will take you to the **Add New Inventory Page**, where you can fill out a form to create a new inventory item.

    ![Fire Shot Capture218 Inventory Display Add Old Client Portal [dashboard Lynxssp Com] Pn](/images/FireShotCapture218-InventoryDisplayAddOld-ClientPortal-[dashboard.lynxssp.com].png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create a DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory Form

When adding a DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) inventory to the Lynx SSP Platform, you'll need to prepare and enter several pieces of information or data into the following form:

![O Pn](/images/o.png)

Based on the form above, here is the information and data required to add a new **DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory**:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Display Name (Required)" iconType="regular" titleSize="p">
    You can enter the **Display Name** or a unique name for your DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-1.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 1 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 1 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="SKU (Required)">
    There are no strict rules for SKU numbering. The format and numbering are entirely up to your company's policy. The SKU can be used as a unique parameter to perform a specific search for an inventory item.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-2.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 2 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 2 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Venue ID (Optional)">
    **Venue ID** is a unique identification code used to mark the location or placement point of an inventory item (like a billboard or DOOH screen) within the Lynx SSP. The Venue ID makes it easier for the system to differentiate one digital screen from another, even if they are in the same area.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-3.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 3 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 3 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Specification (Required)">
    You can select the specification that matches your DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory. (See Specification, Type & Category)

    Example: Choose the Roadside specification.

    ![Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 4 Pn](/images/MenambahkanDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-4.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type (Required)">
    You select the "DOOH" type because you are adding a DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory.

    ![Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 5 Pn](/images/MenambahkanDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-5.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Category (Required)">
    You must select the appropriate category for the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory you are adding.

    For example, choose the Digital Billboard category.

    ![Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 6 Pn](/images/MenambahkanDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-6.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resolution - Width (Required)">
    You can enter the resolution of the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory you are adding.

    For example, if you adding a Digital Billboard with a resolution of 1920 x 1080, you would enter 1920 as the width.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-7.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 7 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 7 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resolution - Height (Required)">
    You can enter the resolution of the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory you are adding.

    For example, if you choose a Digital Billboard with a resolution of 1920 x 1080, you would enter 1080 as the height.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-8.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 8 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 8 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Total Slot (Required)">
    You must enter the total number of slots for the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory you are adding. The number of slots or videos that play on a billboard depends on you as the Media Owner.

    For example, if you select the Digital Billboard category with a total of 5 Slots, then there will be 5 content slots that play in rotation.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-9.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 9 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 9 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Duration per-Spot (Required)">
    You also need to enter the duration per spot for each slot of the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory you're adding. The format should be in seconds.

    For example, if you select the Digital Billboard category with a total of 5 slots and a per-spot duration of 30 seconds, then each of the 5 slots will be displayed for 30 seconds in rotation.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-10.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 10 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 10 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Impression Multiplier (Optional)">
    You can enter the **Impression Multiplier** if the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory you're adding is also available for Programmatic Campaigns.

    The Impression Multiplier is a multiplication factor used to estimate the actual number of impressions from a single ad play on a Digital-Out-of-Home (DOOH) screen, as one play can be seen by multiple people at once.

    For example, if one ad spot has an **Impression Multiplier of 200**, it is estimated that every time the ad appears, it is seen by 200 impression.

    ![Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 11 Pn](/images/MenambahkanDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-11.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Height (Required)">
    You must enter the height of the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory you are adding. The unit used is meters.

    For example, if you choose a Digital Billboard with dimensions of 2.5 m x 1 m and a height from the ground of 3 m, you would enter 1 m as the height.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-12.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 12 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 12 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Width (Required)">
    You must enter the width of the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory you are adding. The unit used is meters.

    For example, if you choose a Digital Billboard with dimensions of 2.5 m x 1 m and a height from the ground of 3 m, you would enter 2.5 m as the width.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-13.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 13 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 13 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Height from Ground (Required)">
    You must enter the height from the ground of the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory you are adding. The unit used is meters.

    For example, if you choose a Digital Billboard with dimensions of 2.5 m x 1 m and a height from the ground of 3 m, you would enter 3 m as the height from the ground.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-14.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 14 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 14 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Location (Latitude & Longitude) (Required)">
    You must enter the location points, including the latitude and longitude, of your Digital-Out-of-Home (DOOH) Inventory.

    For example, if your  Digital Billboard's location is at -6.224694331660831, 106.80518378102471, you would enter those coordinates.

    ![Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 15 Pn](/images/MenambahkanDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-15.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Direction Facing (Optional)">
    It is highly recommended that you set the Direction Facing for your inventory. Direction Facing provides information on which direction your billboard is facing.

    This information adds significant value for potential buyers, as it helps them determine the optimal ad placement for their campaigns.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-16.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 16 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 16 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"34%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Direction Facing Method & Angle (Required)">
    Once you have enabled Direction Facing, you must enter information regarding the method used and its range. This information is crucial for providing accurate data to potential advertisers, allowing them to better evaluate the effectiveness of your ad placement.

    ![Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 17 Pn](/images/MenambahkanDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-17.png)

    You can use either Custom Direction or Cardinal Direction.

    <Accordion title="Custom Direction">
      If you're using Custom Direction, you'll need to enter the Latitude and Longitude of the location, along with the Visibility Distance in meters.

      ![Screenshot2025 10 15075701 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-10-15075701.png)
    </Accordion>
    <Accordion title="Cardinal Direction">
      If using Cardinal Direction, you choose which way the billboard faces, along with the Visibility Distance or its range (in meters).

      ![Screenshot2025 10 15073431 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-10-15073431.png)
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map Location Billboard">
    After entering the Location information (Latitude & Longitude) and the Direction Facing, the map will display the specific location of your DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard.

    ![Screenshot2025 10 15074104 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-10-15074104.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Address Notes (Required)">
    You can fill in this note with the complete address of your inventory location.

    Example: You chose the Digital Billboard at Senayan 500, South Jakarta, Jakarta.

    ![Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 19 Pn](/images/MenambahkanDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-19.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Potential impressions per day (Optional)">
    If you have data on the number of potential impressions per day generated by that inventory, you can fill in this column. If not, you can leave it blank.

    Example: You chose the Digital Billboard with a potential impression of 500,000 per day.

    ![Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 20 Pn](/images/MenambahkanDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-20.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Display Image (Optional)">
    You can upload photos of the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard you are adding to Lynx SSP.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Display Status (Optional)">
    By default, the billboard status is set to False. If you activate this status, the billboard will appear in the inventory list that can be selected when running a campaign and will be available for sale.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-22.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 22 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 22 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"49%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Price (Required)">
    You must enter the monthly price for the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard you are adding to Lynx SSP.

    Example: You chose the Digital Billboard with a price of IDR 100,000,000 per month.

    ![Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 23 Pn](/images/MenambahkanDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-23.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="CPM (Cost per-Mille) (Optional)">
    You can enter the price per thousand impressions for the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard that you are adding to Lynx SSP, if the inventory will also be available for Programmatic Campaigns.

    Example: You chose the Digital Billboard with a price of IDR 100,000 per thousand impressions.

    ![Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 24 Pn](/images/MenambahkanDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-24.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Display Operational Days (Optional)">
    You can select the specific days of operation for the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard you are adding to Lynx SSP. By default, it will be displayed every day.

    <img
      src="/images/MenambahkanDOOH(Digital-Out-of-Home)InventoryForm-25.png"
      alt="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 25 Pn"
      title="Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 25 Pn"
      style={{ width:"50%" }}
      className="mx-auto"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Display Operational Hours (Optional)">
    You can select the specific operational hours for the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory or billboard that you are adding to Lynx SSP. By default, it will be displayed for all 24 hours.

    Example: Ypu chose the Digital Billboard with operational hours from 06:00 to 23:59 only.

    ![Menambahkan Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Form 26 Pn](/images/MenambahkanDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryForm-26.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Save' ">
    After filling in all the information for adding a DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory, press the "Save" button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After completing the steps to create a **DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory**, you can use that inventory in a campaign and media plan to generate a campaign report.
