This page helps you check the running filer in your DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory.

Make sure that the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory in Lynx SSP is connected to the screen. Here are the steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the “Action” Button & Click “Edit”">
    ![Check Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Connection 1 Pn](/images/CheckDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryConnection-1.png)

    This will display the inventory details page.

    ![Check Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Connection 1 2 Pn](/images/CheckDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryConnection-1.2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the “Screen” Tab">
    ![Check Dooh(digital Out Of Home)inventory Connection 2 Pn](/images/CheckDOOH\(Digital-Out-of-Home\)InventoryConnection-2.png)

    Then it will display the Screen Page.

    ![Cek Koneksi Filler Contentpada DOOH Inventory 2 2 Pn](/images/CekKoneksiFillerContentpadaDOOHInventory-2.2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click “Copy URL”">
    ![Cek Koneksi Filler Contentpada DOOH Inventory 3 Pn](/images/CekKoneksiFillerContentpadaDOOHInventory-3.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the URL in a browser tab">
    Then it will display the added filler.

    ![Imaaaage5 Pn](/images/imaaaage5.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
