title: "Creating a Filler Content "
---

This page helps you create new filler content on the Lynx SSP Platform

There are several steps involved in creating filler content, including:

## Accessing the Create New Filler Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the “Create New” Filer Button">
    ![Accessingthe Create New Filler Page 1 Pn](/images/AccessingtheCreateNewFillerPage-1.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create New Filler Content Form

When creating new filler content for the Lynx SSP Platform, you will need to prepare and enter some information or data in the following form:

![Create New Filler Content Form Pn](/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm.png)

Based on the form above, the information or data required to create new filler content is as follows:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Filler Name (Required)">
    You must enter the Name filler or unique name of the filler content you created.

    ![Create New Filler Content Form 1 Pn](/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resolution (Required)">
    You must enter the resolution of the filler content you have created and ensure that the resolution matches the resolution of the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory to be inserted.

    <img
      src="/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-2.png"
      alt="Create New Filler Content Form 2 Pn"
      title="Create New Filler Content Form 2 Pn"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Duration (Required)">
    You must select the duration of the filler content created. The duration can be Always or Select Duration. By default, it will always be displayed.

    ![Create New Filler Content Form 3 Pn](/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-3.png)

    ![Create New Filler Content Form 32 Pn](/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-32.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="List Creative Content (Required)">
    Display a list of creative content to be added.

    ![Create New Filler Content Form 4 Pn](/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-4.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Button Add Creative Content (Required)">
    You can add creative elements to your filler content.

    <img
      src="/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-5.png"
      alt="Create New Filler Content Form 5 Pn"
      title="Create New Filler Content Form 5 Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"20%" }}
    />

    You can choose from the library or list of creative media that has been added or uploaded via computer.

    **Example:** Add the creative media that I had previously added.

    ![Create New Filler Content Form 52 Pn](/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-52.png)

    <p> ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎</p>

    ![Create New Filler Content Form 53 Pn](/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-53.png)

    Then, after adding it, it will display a list of creative media.

    ![Create New Filler Content Form 54 Pn](/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-54.png)

    You can rearrange the order of creatives and delete them from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="List Connect to Display (Required)">
    Display a list of (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory that will be linked to filler content.

    ![Create New Filler Content Form 6 Pn](/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-6.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Display (Required)">
    You can connect the filler content you create to DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory.

    **Example:** You entered the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory that I added earlier ([See Creating a DOOH Inventory ↗](/guideline/screen-connection/html/create-dooh-inventory/create))

    ![Create New Filler Content Form 72 Pn](/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-72.png)

    <p> ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎</p>

    ![Create New Filler Content Form 73 Pn](/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-73.png)

    Then, after adding it, it will display the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory list.

    ![Create New Filler Content Form 74 Pn](/images/CreateNewFillerContentForm-74.png)

    You can unbind or disconnect from the DOOH (Digital-Out-of-Home) Inventory that has been connected.
  </Step>
  <Step title=" Click the “Submit” Button">
    After filling in all the information and adding filler content, click the “**Submit**” button.
  </Step>
</Steps>
